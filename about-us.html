About Leaders Academy

Our Vision, Our Mission, Our Story

Vision

Our Vision

We envision a world where justice, peace, and prosperity prevail, led by capable, courageous, compassionate individuals and organisations.

Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop those leaders and their organisations through world-class consulting, training, and coaching programs.

Our Story

Igniting the spark of potential

Our story began over two decades ago with a passion and a mission: igniting the spark of potential by helping people find their voice to share their stories and experiences. We started by offering personal development training, coaching, and mentoring to the youth.

Since those early days, our commitment to empowering people has only grown stronger. We've joined forces with businesses, schools, and community groups to guide hundreds of individuals from all walks of life toward personal growth, impactful leadership, and personal transformation.

The impact of our work extends far beyond the training. Many of the people we've had the privilege to work with have gone on to pursue successful careers in law, engineering, medicine, and more. Some are now business owners, executives, professionals, and proud parents, passing on the confidence and skills they acquired to their teams and children.

Join us in our mission to help people find their voice, as we continue to make a difference, one speaker at a time.

Our Team

Leadership, By Example

Abdul Fattah

Consultant | Adviser | Leadership Coach | Trainer

Abdul is a dynamic and visionary leader and senior project manager with over 15 years of experience in strategic management, digital transformation, and team leadership. Specialising in project management, change management, leadership coaching, and corporate training, Abdul excels in guiding organisations through complex transformations and developing high-performing teams.

His expertise spans multiple industries, including Retail Pharmacy, Finance, Government, Not-For-Profit, and Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG). Abdul's extensive experience in implementing strategic initiatives and fostering a culture of continuous improvement has consistently driven business success.

He holds certifications as a Change Management Practitioner (CMP), Professional Scrum Master (PSM), and in ITIL Service Management (ITIL SM). Abdul is passionate about empowering leaders and teams to reach their full potential, ensuring sustainable growth and positive organisational change.
